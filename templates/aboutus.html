Challenges Faced by Stray Dogs

Over 60 Million Stray Dogs in India

India has more than 60 million stray dogs, with 77% of the population encountering a stray dog at least once a week.

Source: VOA News

High Risk of Road Traffic Accidents

Stray dogs account for 69% of animal-related road traffic accidents, leading to significant injuries and fatalities.

Source: PMC Article

Global Stray Dog Population

The World Health Organization estimates there are nearly 200 million stray dogs worldwide, many suffering from health issues due to lack of monitoring.

Source: FOUR PAWS International

Public Health Concerns

The overpopulation of stray dogs is a serious public health and animal welfare concern, contributing to environmental pollution and transmission of zoonotic diseases.

Source: PMC Article
